What is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer is a disease where cells in the breast grow out of control. It's the most common cancer in women worldwide and the second most common cancer overall.

Why Early Detection Matters

Early detection of breast cancer is crucial. When caught early, breast cancer is more likely to be treated successfully. Regular screenings and advanced detection tools can save lives.

How Our Tool Helps

Our AI-powered tool analyzes breast ultrasound images to classify potential abnormalities. It aids in early detection by providing quick, accurate assessments to support healthcare professionals.
